# Financial Markets Notes

## 1. Market Structure
* **Primary Market:** New securities issued (IPOs).
* **Secondary Market:** Existing securities traded (NYSE, NASDAQ).

## 2. Efficient Market Hypothesis (EMH)
* **Weak Form:** Prices reflect all past trading data.
* **Semi-Strong Form:** Prices reflect all publicly available info.
* **Strong Form:** Prices reflect all public and private info.

## 3. Asset Pricing Models
* **CAPM (Capital Asset Pricing Model):**
    Defines the relationship between risk and expected return.
    $$E(R_i) = R_f + \beta_i [E(R_m) - R_f]$$
    * $R_f$: Risk-free rate.
    * $\beta$: Sensitivity to market movements (Systematic Risk). 
    * $E(R_m) - R_f$: Market Risk Premium.

## 4. Fixed Income
* **Bond Pricing:** Inverse relationship between interest rates and bond prices.
* **Yield to Maturity (YTM):** Total return anticipated on a bond if held until it matures.
